Mission Statement

Nexera Medical's mission is to protect lives through advanced antimicrobial technologies and high-quality medical products that improve personal protection, strengthen domestic manufacturing capabilities, and enhance national preparedness for infectious disease, public health emergencies, and future biological threats.


Corporate Overview

Headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, Florida

Nexera Medical is an innovator in infection-fighting respiratory protection. Our flagship SpectraShield™ antimicrobial respirators combine NIOSH-certified filtration with embedded copper-silver zeolite technology to kill 99% of bacteria and viruses. FDA-cleared for extended multi-patient use and 8-hour-cleared, reusable, our masks help reduce waste and costs while improving safety.

All our antimicrobial products are manufactured using Fosshield®, a patented technology that embeds a non-toxic silver- and copper-zeolite into a fabric's fibers. Our products are vital not only for protecting individuals from infectious disease but also for mitigating the spread of disease. We are partnering with governments and leaders in the health care industry to prepare for future pandemics and build sustainable capacity in the United States. Our portfolio of SpectraShield™ antimicrobial respirator masks is our first step in protecting emergency responders, health professionals, and the public against such threats.


Nexera Medical is a subsidiary of LifeProtect.
