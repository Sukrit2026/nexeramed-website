Respirator Masks

With tested filtration performance equivalent to an N99 (meaning 99% of contaminants are trapped in the filter and do not get through the mask), the SpectraShield™ mask meets the guidelines for infectious disease exposure control established by the World Health Organization ("WHO") and the Center for Disease Control ("CDC").

The SpectraShield™ is scientifically proven to combat the growth of harmful microorganisms and biological pathogens and should be considered the number one choice in personal protection. Every family should have a stockpile of SpectraShield™ masks at home.

Some antimicrobial masks require frequent changing of the filtration cartridges at an additional cost where this is not necessary with the SpectraShield™ mask. Easily and hands-down, no other antimicrobial mask can compare to the performance of the SpectraShield™ Series of mask.
